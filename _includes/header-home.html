What is M3J?

M3J is a free math modeling experience hosted over Zoom from April 24th to April 25th.

Do I have to have prior experience with math modeling?

You don't need background knowledge of math modeling to join! In fact, our team is working on getting resources to help you along the way and we hope that the event will be a learning experience for everyone.
